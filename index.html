Dark Chocolate Crunch Mug Cake Recipe
By Chef Laura Briscoe
Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 90 seconds Servings: Serves 1

Ingredients

- 2 T (1 oz) unsalted butter (plant based ok)

- 2 T brown sugar or brown sugar substitute

- 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate Crunch, Laura's Gourmet Granola

- 1/4 cup Chocolate Protein powder

- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

- pinch of Kosher salt

- 1/4 cup vanilla almond milk, unsweetened

- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

- 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Place granola into a food processor and crush until fine. Set aside.
  2. Place butter into a microwave safe mug and melt on medium power for 45 seconds
  3. Add sugar and stir to combine
  4. Add crushed granola, protein powder, baking powder, salt, almond milk and vanilla extract and stir until well combined, smooth and lump free.
  5. Stir in all but 1 T of the chocolate chips. Top the batter with the remaining chocolate chips.
  6. Microwave for 90 seconds, then hold for another minute. Allow to cool slightly before enjoying.

Notes
Best hot, warm or room temperature. Do not refrigerate.
